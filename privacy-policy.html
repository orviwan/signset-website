Introduction

Welcome to the SignSet website (the "Website"). At SignSet, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices regarding your personal information, please contact us at info@signset.co.uk.

By accessing or using our Website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data to improve the Website's performance and user experience. This includes:

  • Automatically Collected Data:
    • IP addresses
    • Browser type
    • Device information
    • Website usage data (via Google Analytics)
  • Collected Through Third-Party Tools: Google Ads and Google Analytics for marketing and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Website Performance: To understand how users interact with our Website and improve user experience.
  • Analytics: To gather insights about Website traffic, audience behavior, and performance metrics using Google Analytics.

We do not use your data for profiling or automated decision-making.

3. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We share user data with the following third-party services for the purposes described above:

4. User Rights

As a user in the UK and EU, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Access: Request details about the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Erasure: Request that we delete your personal data where legally applicable.
  • Restriction of Processing: Request limited use of your data.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your personal data in a commonly used format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@signset.co.uk.

5. Data Security

We do not store or process sensitive personal data. Data collected is stored securely within the services provided by Google. However, no data transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Analyze user behavior on the Website.

For more details on how cookies work and how to manage them, please visit our Cookie Policy.

7. International Data Transfers

Data collected through Google Analytics may be transferred to and stored in countries outside the UK or EU. Google complies with international data transfer standards, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).
